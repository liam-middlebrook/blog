PawPrints Adlibs

20 Nov 2014

At the beginning of the summer for the National Day of Civic Hacking I worked on a project that took petitions from the White House's We the People website and remixed them up with adlibs. Much like the Mad Libs style game. I've posted the code for that up on GitHub.

A few months ago RIT opened up a new petition service for Student Government that they call PawPrints. Last week I decided to update and remix my wethepeople-adlibs python script and make it use PawPrints. pawprints-adlibs is on GitHub.
