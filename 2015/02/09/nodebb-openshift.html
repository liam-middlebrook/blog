Deploying NodeBB To OpenShift

09 Feb 2015

A few weeks ago one of my friends asked me about the possibility of setting up online forum software for a community that he's part of. He also had mentioned that he wanted to avoid using any Forum Software that was written in PHP. We also wanted to make sure they we had a free hosting solution, so we went with OpenShift and NodeBB.

I found the NodeBB instructions for deploying to OpenShift here. After looking through this guide and attempting it a few times I was having issues with getting NodeBB to publicly run. I found the issue was related to NodeBB's Express instance not binding to the proper host. After looking through some of the source I found that I was missing bind_address in my config file, so I submitted a pull request.
