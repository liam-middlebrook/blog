Frog Fractions 2

02 Mar 2015

When I was at GDC I stopped by the IGF booth and saw a sign labeled "Frog Fractions 2." When I walked up to the booth I saw what appeared to be a golfing game. I stood behind someone for about 10 minutes, I was pretty confused, waiting for the change in gameplay that I remember from the original Frog Fractions.
