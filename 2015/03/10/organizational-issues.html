What / Where

Legal Primer for FOSS: Chapter 3

The Gist

The chapter describes the different forms that FOSS projects can exist in. It covers the basic duties of each of those properties from corporations to sole proprietors.

The Good

  • Good overview of different corporate entity types

  • Easily Grokable

  • Great walkthrough on the incorporation process

The Bad

  • Social issues still persist

  • No overview of different non-corporate entity types

The Questions

  • How to manage liability?

  • What other positions/roles are part of this process?

In Review

Solid read, definitely would recommend to anyone starting their own FOSS project that has potential for money-dollars. 9/10
